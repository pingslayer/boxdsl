# Client Feature List: Simple EMS
**Product Status**: Core MVP Requirements

## 1. Employee Profile Management
The system must allow HR staff to perform the following actions:
- **Register New Employees**: Capture full name, unique business email (mandatory), join date, and initial role.
- **Profile Updates**: Allow modifications to an employee's department, job title, and contact information.
- **Service Status tracking**: Mark employees as "Active", "On Leave", or "Terminated" with historical dates.

## 2. Organizational View
- **Employee Directory**: A searchable table view of all active employees.
- **Quick Search**: A global search bar to find employees by name or email address immediately.
- **Department Filtering**: Ability to filter the directory by common departments (e.g., Engineering, HR, Sales).

## 3. Administrative Control
- **Department Management**: Create and rename company departments.
- **Role Assignment**: Dynamic assignment of roles to employees.

---
*Implementation must ensure that all features are accessible via a clean, intuitive dashboard.*
