# Business Objectives: Simple Employee Management System (EMS)
**Client**: Internal HR Department
**Date**: April 13, 2026

## 1. Problem Statement
The current method of tracking employee data via fragmented spreadsheets and physical files has led to significant data redundancy, slow onboarding times, and difficulty in generating accurate organizational reports. There is a lack of a "Single Source of Truth" for staff records.

## 2. Business Goals
By implementing this Simple EMS, the client aims to achieve:
- **Centralized Data Management**: A single, secure database for all employee-related information.
- **Operational Efficiency**: Streamlined hiring and profile update workflows for the HR team.
- **Improved Data Integrity**: Elimination of duplicate records and manual entry errors.
- **Secure Access**: Ensuring that sensitive employee information is only accessible to authorized personnel.

## 3. Key Success Metrics
The success of this system will be measured by:
- A **30% reduction** in time taken to complete new employee onboarding.
- **100% elimination** of duplicate employee records within the first month.
- Positive feedback from the HR team regarding the ease of use of the interface.
